Gear

You don't need a lot of stuff to surf. Only a surfboard, a wetsuit, and a leash. In colder months, you will need booties, gloves and a hood. There are several good surf shops around the city and Long Island that will sell you all the gear, but you can also order it online.

Wetsuits

In warm weather, you can surf in your bathing suit. As the water and air temps get colder, you'll want to wear either a wetsuit top, spring suit, or full wetsuit.
