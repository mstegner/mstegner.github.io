Beach 92nd Street, Rockaway: Rockaway's main surfing beach is just blocks from the A train and perhaps New York's most crowded breaks. It's also one of the more dangerous and challenging waves-steep, fast, and often unpredictable. The congestion can make the natives (or those claiming to be natives) testy.
