Lessons

If you are new to surfing, definitely consider taking a couple of lessons before setting out to catch your own waves. There are many great surfing schools throughout New York and Long Island, some of which teach lessons year-round. Most of these schools provide you with a wetsuit, surfboard and leash, plus many good tips to help you get the hang of it.


In a typical group lesson, you will learn:

  • how and where to lay on the board
  • how to pop up
  • surf etiquette
  • how to paddle against the waves
  • how to catch a wave
  • how to wipe out safely

LOCALS SURF SCHOOL

localssurfschool.com

Locals Surf School was founded in 2011 by Mike Reinhardt and Mike Kololyan, two life-long surfer/skaters and locals of Rockaway Beach, NYC. They offer private and group surf and skate lessons, camps for kids, charity programs and contests.

SKUDIN SURF SCHOOL

skudinsurf.com

A family-run surf school that offers group and private surf lessons for kids and adults, plus summer camps and contests. Lessons offered in Long Beach, Rockaway and Rincon, Puerto Rico.

NEW YORK SURF SCHOOL

surflessonsnewyork101.com

Based at Beach 69th Street in Rockaway, certified instructor Frank Cullen and his crew offer group and private lessons to kids and adults, plus summer camps.
