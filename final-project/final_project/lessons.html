Lessons

If you are new to surfing, definitely consider taking a couple of lessons before setting out to catch your own waves. There are many great surfing schools throughout New York and Long Island, some of which teach lessons year-round. Most of these schools provide you with a wetsuit, surfboard and leash, plus many good tips to help you get the hang of it.


In a typical group lesson, you will learn:

  • how and where to lay on the board
  • how to walk your board into the water
  • how to manage your board while walking out against the surf
  • how to paddle against the waves
  • how to catch a wave
  • how to wipe out safely
