I Surf New York

Hi there. Thanks for visiting! Do you surf? Would you like to know more about surfing in New York? I put this site together as a guide for anyone who would like to pick up surfing, but also as a fun web dev project to practice new skills.

I have included some videos and songs you can listen to while navigating my site, for a more immersive experience. Oh, there's also some info on New York's best surfing beaches and surf schools, and some inspiration to get you excited to ride some waves!

essential surf lingo you should know

kook

A beginner or someone who is not very good at surfing

grom

A young or inexperienced surfer... Usually a child who's learning to surf.

hang ten

When you're riding a longboard with both feet on the nose of the board.

Noah

A shark!

foamie

A longboard made of foam. Great surfboard type for a beginner.

barrel

Where the wave is hollow while it's breaking. Also called a tube.

party wave

A wave surfed by several people at once.

line up

The place just outside the breaking waves where surfers wait for their waves.

pop up

Describes the move a surfer makes to go from lying on the surfboard to standing in position to ride a wave.
