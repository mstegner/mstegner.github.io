essential surf lingo

kook

A beginner or someone who is not very good at surfing

grom

A young or inexperienced surfer. Usually a child who's learning to surf.

hang ten

Riding a longboard with both feet on the nose of the board.

Noah

A shark!

foamie

A longboard made of foam. Great surfboard type for beginners.

barrel

Where the wave is hollow while it's breaking. Also called a tube.

party wave

A wave surfed by several people at once.

line up

The place just outside breaking waves where surfers wait for their turn.

pop up

Going from lying on the surfboard to standing in position to ride a wave.
